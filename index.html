About RHTMDAAJ

RHTMDAAJ is a friendship group formed by the 9th-grade students of SMP Sanmarino. We are all about having fun, supporting each other, and making the most out of our school years. Whether it's studying together or getting into funny mischief, we always have each other's backs. Join us as we navigate through the craziness of being a middle school student!
